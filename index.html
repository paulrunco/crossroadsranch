Cross Roads Ranch

Crossroads Ranch, located in Penngrove, California, offers a unique daycare experience in a beautiful ranch setting. A perfect blend of outdoor play, horsemanship, with crafts and activities, surrounded by our many farm animal and loving pets.

Crossroads Ranch and Day Care is a space for kids to experience meaningful challenges and memorable opportunities to make connections with our horses and other animals. We organize activities to foster kids' natural instincts and honor their interconnection with nature and with each other.

Our Ranch is a place where students of all ages, races, backgrounds, and abilities can experience feeling welcome and safe outdoors.
